About SoneMC

SoneMC is my next project focused on Minecraft plugins. I'm focusing on creating plugins that weren't there, or at least I didn't know existed. My primary focus is on fun and survival, but we'll see what's next.

All my plugins are free on every single Minecraft server and probably will remain so.

Of course, if you download a plugin, you automatically confirm that you have read the rules and know what you can and cannot do.
