Frequently Asked Questions

How do I install plugins?

To install plugins, download the .jar file and place it in your server's plugins folder. Then start/restart your server and enjoy.

Are your plugins compatible with Paper/Spigot/Bukkit?

Yes, most of our plugins are compatible with Paper, Spigot, and Bukkit server implementations.

How can I report a bug?

You can report bugs by opening an issue on our GitHub repository or by contacting us in our Discord server.

Do your plugins work on the latest Minecraft version?

Yes, our priority are the newest Minecraft versions.
